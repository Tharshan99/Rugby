Welcome to Jaffna Rugby Club

Jaffna Rugby Club is more than a team; it’s a community built on passion, discipline, and determination. We strive to promote rugby in Jaffna, nurture talent, and inspire the next generation of players. Whether on or off the field, we stand together, united by the love of the Game.

Rise as One

Jaffna Rugby Club – Legacy in Action, Victory in Heart

Founded in November 2018 by Mr. Tilak Duleep Kumar Jebadundaram, Jaffna Rugby Club was built on passion, pride, and a deep love for the game. More than just a team, we are a community united by dedication, resilience, and the pursuit of excellence. As we continue our journey, Jaffna Rugby Club stands as a symbol of hope and inspiration, carrying the spirit of rugby in every match we play.

Stronger Together, Inspired by Legacy

At Jaffna Rugby Club, we blend tradition with ambition, creating a space where athletes grow in skill, character, and camaraderie. With deep roots in Jaffna’s proud heritage, we welcome players of all levels to be part of something bigger—an enduring legacy of strength, unity, and excellence.

Rooted in the heart of Jaffna, the Jaffna Rugby Club is dedicated to igniting a passion for rugby across all generations, fostering teamwork, discipline, and resilience both on and off the field. With a strong commitment to developing homegrown talent, the club nurtures skilled players and instills a culture of excellence, representing Jaffna with pride on national and international platforms. Every match is a testament to the heart, resilience, and determination of our community as we push boundaries and strive for greatness. Through unwavering dedication, Jaffna Rugby Club continues to elevate the sport, empowering athletes and inspiring the next generation. Our vision goes beyond the game—we aim to uplift Jaffna’s rugby legacy and establish the region as a powerhouse in the global rugby arena. By competing at the highest levels, we not only strengthen our players but also create opportunities that unite and empower our community through the spirit of rugby.

Director

Professor Dr. Tham Nimal-Raj – Owner & Director, Jaffna Rugby Club

Professor Dr. Tham Nimal-Raj is a distinguished leader, entrepreneur, and senior NHS General Practitioner based in the UK. With a wealth of experience in both the medical and business sectors, he brings visionary leadership and strategic direction to the Jaffna Rugby Club as its Founder and Director.

Professor Dr. Tham Nimal-Raj is a distinguished leader, entrepreneur, and senior NHS General Practitioner based in the UK. With a wealth of experience in both the medical and business sectors, he brings visionary leadership and strategic direction to the Jaffna Rugby Club as its Founder and Director. Driven by a passion for sports and community development, he founded the club to nurture local talent and promote excellence in the sport.

Beyond rugby, Professor Nimal-Raj is an influential figure in medical education, corporate healthcare, and strategic development. He is the Managing Director of Harley Street Medical Consultants and Lakeside Medical Diagnostic Services and serves as the Provost and Professor of Family Medicine and Anatomy at New Vision University, Tbilisi, Georgia.
